Format

The event will be run as a two day Open Space.

After last year's feedback we decided to do without any set sessions and focus on the power of the participants. You can get further impressions of an Open Space here.

To get an impression of what you will expect and especially what you can do in advance have a look at: unconference.net

Facilitation will be in English. However, it will be possible to run sessions in other languages.

Main Event

August 1-3 2013

The SoCraTes conference will take place from the evening of Thursday August 1st 2013 (about 5pm) through the evening on Saturday August 3rd 2013. So we will have 48 hours to collaborate and share.

Additional Code Retreat

August 4 2013

The main event will be followed by a Code Retreat on Sunday (until about 6pm). This will be a separate, optional event which is not included in the conference.

Important note: Participation in the code retreat will cause additional accommodation costs!

Housekeeping

Rooms

The facility offers about 50 rooms, most of which are twin rooms. An important aspect of SoCraTes is its closedness where everything happens in the same location. Therefore, we can only accept as many participants as there are beds available. When all of the single bed rooms are taken, everybody who requests a single room will transform one of the twin rooms into a single bed room, reducing the number of available slots by one. Therefore we strongly encourage that you choose the "shared room" option during registration. And, once you come to think about it, there are very few reasons to spend much time in your room apart from sleeping and the occasional shower -- which, thanks to the Open Space format, you can take whenever it is the right time for you to do so ;)

When you register, you can let us know whom you'd be willing to share a room with. Even if you don't know anybody just yet, we will do all we can to find a suitable roommate for you. After registration there will also be a wiki for participants to get to know each other and arrange things.

The total price for the conference participation, two overnight stays, meals and coffee will not exceed 250 Euros. The exact amount depends on the number of sponsors we will be able to attract. If we find enough sponsors the conference will cost less than 200.- Euros.

Kolpingstraße 1, 63867 Johannesberg, Germany

Registration

Registration for the event will open late April.

If you want to be informed as soon as the registration opens, you can

  • Join Softwerkskammer, where we will send an invitation mail to all registered members about one week in advance of the registration opening,
  • Follow us on Twitter. We will announce the registration opening there a few days in advance or
  • Contact us directly via email. Please consider this option only if the other options are absolutely inconvenient to you.

During registration you will be asked to answer some questions about your relation to Software Craftsmanship, what you want to share and your expectations for the event. Please be prepared to give answers to these questions.
