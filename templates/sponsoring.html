About the event

The fourth annual Software Craftsmanship and Testing Conference will take place from 7th to 10th August 2014 in Soltau near Hamburg, Germany.

This year, up to 200 people can participate - we scale up after two years of being fully booked.

SoCraTes is an international, non-profit conference, that's community-driven and low-price. A two-day OpenSpace will be followed by a Coderetreat and other workshops.
