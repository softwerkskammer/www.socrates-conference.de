SoCraTes Conference 2012

International Software Craftsmanship and Testing Conference in Germany

Your heart beats for Software Craftsmanship?
You want to actively participate in the community?

What?

This event is about the sustainable creation of useful software in a responsible way.

It consists of two days of highly collaborative interactions, including Lightning Talks (on Thursday evening), scheduled sessions (on Friday), and a self-organized Open Space (on Saturday). The event will be much like a retreat.
We will be there for 48 hours to collaborate and share ideas.

Furthermore, the conference is followed by a CodeRetreat on Sunday August 5th.

Follow @socrates_2012 on Twitter, to stay up-to-date.

When?

August 2-5 2012

More precisely:
The SoCraTes conference takes place from the evening of Thursday August 2nd 2012 (about 5pm) through the evening on Saturday August 4th 2012.
The conference is followed by a Code Retreat on Sunday August 5th 2012 (until about 6pm). This is a separate, optional event which is not included in the conference.

Who should come?

Everyone who is concerned with coding, testing, code quality, software craftsmanship...

  • For you, coding is more than writing text in an editor.
  • For you, testing is more than ensuring quality.
  • For you, testing is more than executing given scenarios.

Where?

Out in the country, at the lovely Seminarzentrum Rückersbach (sorry, german website only), only one hour from Frankfurt (Main) airport.

There you will get your room and the event will happen there. We will rent the whole facility, so we can concentrate on what we like to do most.

Venue Address:
Kolpingstraße 1, 63867 Johannesberg (Google Maps)

How?

Run as a non-profit, low-cost, community-organized event. Brought to you as a joint-effort of all Softwerkskammer groups, the community of all German Software Craftsmanship groups.

Facilitation will be in English, sessions in other languages are welcome.
